Hi, I am Mengdi

I design to inspire.

Hi, I’m Mengdi! I'm currently working with the End User Computing team at VMware as an UX designer. Before this, I was with the Applications User Experience team at Oracle as an interaction designer. I graduated from the University of Michigan School of Information in 2015, specializing in HCI.

My goal as a designer is to leverage design to inspire, empower and engage the user with delightful experience and better relationships.

I enjoy crafting elegant interfaces; I’m also trying to step back and see the whole picture to enhance the overall user experience. My favorite part of design is that I could always collaborate with users and the multi-functional team throughout the decision-making process.

When I’m not building applications and websites, I can be found trying different craft and art formats - see some of my watercolor paintings, doodles, digital paintings, wood burnings, hand-lettering, sketchnotes, generative art, and needle felting here!

Want to get in touch? Feel free to drop me a line or download my resume.
